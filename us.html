Establishment and Foundation


Didital Future IT University was founded in 2008 as a response to the growing demand for specialized education in the field of Information Technology. It was envisioned as an institution that would bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, catering to the evolving technological landscape.

Visionary Leadership


Dr. Nurzhan Doszhanov, a distinguished leader in the field of Information Technology, was one of the pioneering founders of DFIT University. His visionary approach and extensive experience in academia and industry laid the groundwork for the university's establishment.

Commitment to Excellence


With an unwavering commitment to excellence, Dr. Nurzhan Doszhanov spearheaded the recruitment of a talented faculty and staff, fostering an environment that prioritized research, innovation, and the holistic development of students. His leadership ensured that the university became a beacon of technological advancement in the region.

Early Years and Growth


In its nascent years, Didital Future IT University began with a modest infrastructure and a handful of dedicated faculty members. However, through a commitment to excellence and innovation, it quickly gained recognition for its cutting-edge curriculum and industry-aligned programs.

Academic Excellence


Today,DFIT University stands as a leading institution renowned for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and research in Information Technology. The university continues to evolve its curriculum to meet the demands of the digital era while fostering an environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving.

Student-Centric Approach


At Didital Future IT University, students are at the heart of everything we do. We provide a supportive and inclusive learning environment, fostering a culture of diversity, collaboration, and personal growth. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to guiding and mentoring students throughout their academic journey.
